Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At InvesTracker, we prioritize the privacy and security of users' personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how data collected through the mobile application is stored, processed, used, and protected.

2. Data We Collect

The application processes personal information such as:

  • Name and surname
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • User-declared financial data (foreign currency, gold, cryptocurrency assets, and debt information)

3. How We Use Your Data

Data is securely stored and used solely for:

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Application functionality and financial tracking services
  • Service continuity and improvement

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on secure servers located in Turkey. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Data is not shared with third parties nor transferred domestically or internationally, except when required by law.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained as long as your account is active. Users may request account or data deletion at any time, and all data will be permanently erased upon such request.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request information about your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request restriction of data processing

7. Contact Us

For any questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us at:

Email: investrackerapp@gmail.com

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be announced through the application.
